Title: Async workflows in Clojure

Type: 40min talk

Tags: programming-methodology programming-async programming-framework

Abstract:

Status: maybe next time

The talk will present the various options of achieving truly async inner-process communication within services.

  1. Intro to CSP and the Actor Model
  2. presenting various async workflow libs in Clojure:
    2.a. core.async
    2.b. pulsar and quasar
    2.c. manifold (deferred)
  3. Present common problems and use cases and their proposed solution and modelling while utilizing the ideas and libs presented in section 1 and 2.
